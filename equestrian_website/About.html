About

All About Us!

Colm started out riding when he was just eight years old, when he quickly developed a love for the sport. The reason Colm set up his own livery yard is because he wanted to create an environment in which horses could be given the best care, love and attention they deserve. He has owned and ran his own livery yard now for four years, because he loves working with horses and building good relationships with his clients.

“I’ve been on livery with Colm now for a year! It’s really good, great value and my horse is well looked after . the atmosphere is very homey and like a big family and everyone cares so much about all of the horses, it’s a real team effort!”
Bobbie Hickey
“I’ve been on livery with Colm with my pony Willow now for about a year and a half. My dad also keeps his horse, Apache, with Colm. Being on livery with Colm is really fun, I’ve made so many friends in the yard. Colm has taught me for two and a half years now and myself and Willow have progressed greatly!”
Seersha Watson
