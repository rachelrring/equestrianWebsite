Training With Colm

Colm started his competitive riding career at sixteen years old in jumping. He continued to compete in jumping up until he was twenty-four years old at which point he started his dressage career. He’s currently competing at Advanced Medium with his two horses and hopes to school them both up to Advanced very soon!

Colm teaches a wide range of clients, from young to old all with mixed abilities.
“The best thing about teaching for me is watching each of my students progress within their chosen discipline.”
